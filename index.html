Who are we?

A small team of enthusiasts who want to solve the recurring problem of having to choose between ecology and design.

From the creation to the end of life of our lights, our mantra is ecology, aesthetics and durability in order to enable a responsible, practical AND aesthetic purchase to our customers!

Each lamp is eco-friendly & handmade

The pieces of wood (all unique) are collected, highlighted and then assembled by hand. Each lamp is thus a work of art, so that the emotion of the sea is transcribed into light in your living room.
